Blue Gold Macaw Parrot

The Blue and Gold Macaw has a very friendly personality and does very well as a family pet

  • SCIENTIFIC NAME: Ara ararauna
  • AVERAGE LENGTH: 33 to 34 inches
  • AVEAGE LIFE EXPECTANCY: 40 years
  • HABITAL: Tropical forests near rivers and swamps

Blue and Gold macaws have naked white facial disc along with the fine lines of green feathers, with green crown and forehead. They have yellow eyes, large beak, and black throat. These macaws have long been admired for their remarkable appearance and their aptitude as a great talking bird.it can learn a vocabulary of around 20 words and phrases
